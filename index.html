üìñ Course Overview

This course provides a rigorous foundation in the mathematics of computation, covering fundamental topics in logic, computability theory, complexity theory, and graph theory. Students will develop skills in formal reasoning, proof techniques, and algorithmic analysis essential for advanced computer science.
